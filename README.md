## Official Expedition Archive

**Expedition Leader:** Sir Thomas Mitchell, Colonial Explorer

**Primary Objective:** To chart the course of the Victoria River, assess the surrounding lands for pastoral and agricultural potential, and identify routes for future settlement and development.

### Introduction

This repository serves as the official archival record of the Victoria River Expedition, conducted under the auspices of the Colonial Office. The expedition successfully navigated and mapped the Victoria River, discovering extensive tracts of fertile pastoral land and overcoming significant geographical obstacles. The findings documented herein are intended for colonial planning and historical preservation.

All official reports, field notes, and supporting documents shall be maintained in this archive according to the established protocol for formal submission.
